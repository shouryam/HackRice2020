Clear patterns of healthcare inequity exist along racial lines. However, the severity of these trends may be hard to put into perspective. The mission of this website is to provide all individuals with quantitative information to better understand the breadth and depth of this healthcare inequity crisis. When more individuals become aware of how race affects healthcare outcomes, we can add urgency to the movement for greater equality. Please feel free to look around for resources you can use to become an ally!
